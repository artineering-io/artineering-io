Our Process

Every stylized production is unique and needs to be thoughtfully planned and executed to match your desired aesthetic and pipeline.

  1. Send us an email introducing yourself and what you have in mind. We'll arrange for a personal meeting to get to know each other and nerd out about pixels, your project, workflow and how we can make it a reality together.

  2. Based on your project's unique requirements and concept art, we create a thorough style breakdown. We list all visual characteristics, how they should behave in motion, potential approaches and a timeline to make it all happen. Once approved, we draft a contract and begin development.

  3. Our development is done in iterative stages to facilitate an effective feedback loop. You will get to test the software at each stage with your assets and pipeline to steer our development towards your exact production needs.

  4. Once all requested features have been developed and the software is working well in production, you will get the production-proven release, including tutorials and its documentation. We want our software to work well for you, so you will continue receiving support from us.

  5. Stylized rendering offers unlimited possibilities! We would love to continue working with you developing new styles, additional features or new requirements to complement our previous work and augment your productions.
