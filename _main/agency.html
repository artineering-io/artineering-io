Our Process

Every good production, especially while pioneering outstanding styles, requires good communication and care during its planning and execution. To make this journey as smooth as possible, we follow these basic steps.

  1. Every project and workflow is unique and we want to provide you with an ideal solution. As our tools are always highly customized, we want to get to know you first to make sure our tools fit into your current workflow and will enable you to chase your wildest NPR dreams.

  2. Together we make a list of your requirements, break down the concept art and style you want to achieve and come up with a potential approach and timeline to reach our set goals.

  3. After testing our options for the most suitable approach, we will dig deeper into making the optimal approach a reality. Throughout the process, we keep you posted and will provide you with several version releases (Alpha, Beta, release candidate) for you to test and give us constant feedback.

  4. Once the earlier set requirements are met and your vision of a brand-new style and workflow came to reality, you will receive a final version, including documentation from us. Rest assured, we will keep supporting you whenever needed.
